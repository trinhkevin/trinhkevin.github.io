WHO AM I?

Hi, my name is Kevin Trinh, and I hail from the rainy city of Portland, OR.

I am a graduate of the University of Notre Dame majoring in Computer Science. I am interested in Application Development, Cloud Computing, and Computer Security. I am currently working at Journera as a Software Developer.

In my free time, I go to the gym, play basketball, and peruse Reddit more than I should. I have one dog, named Bella, who is a fiesty chihuahua, and I am hoping to get a cat sometime in the future.

I hope to continue developing applications and software that help and improve peoples' lives. I firmly believe that technology can improve millions of lives and I am excited to help usher in a new era of technological achievement.
